Engineering & Validation

System Integration & Quality

Corner Module Engineering

We design for the Corner Module: the harmonious interaction between Suspension, Steering, and Braking. Every component is validated as a complete integrated system — not isolated replacement parts. This maintains original kinematic geometry for safe handling.

Safety Factor > 3×

Steering parts are safety-critical. FRAP components are designed with a safety factor exceeding 3×, verified by in-house FEM and FEA analysis. Every critical dimension is validated against OEM CAD data — not reverse-engineered from worn samples.

Validation Protocols

FEA & FEM Modeling

Finite Element Analysis on virtual 3D CAD prototypes before forging. Material redistributed to high-stress zones for optimal strength-to-weight ratios.

4-Axis Dynamic Bench

Vertical, lateral, longitudinal, and torsional loads simultaneously. Simulates potholes and off-road at 10Hz cyclic fatigue rates to destruction.

100% Eddy Current

Every critical forged part undergoes mandatory Eddy Current or Magnetoscope inspection detecting sub-surface micro-cracks invisible to the eye.

500h+ Salt Spray

Premium Zinc Flake / E-Coat subjected to 500+ hours continuous salt-spray testing per ASTM B117, validating resistance to winter road chemicals.
